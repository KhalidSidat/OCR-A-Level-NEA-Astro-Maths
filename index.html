Instructions

Solve Math Problems as quickly as you can and aim for the highest score
In Survival Mode you play until the projectiles reach your spaceship
In the 5 minutes mode you play until the timer ends or projectiles reach your spaceship
Watch out for tricky questions and keep an eye out for Power-Ups

Controls

A and D or Left and Right Arrow Keys
Spacebar to Shoot

Power-Ups

Clock - Slows down incoming projectiles for 10 seconds
Freeze - Freezes the rockets for 5 seconds
Double points - Doubles your points for 10 seconds
